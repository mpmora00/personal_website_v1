    • A little bit about me...

    I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Dartmouth College in 2022 with a major in Computer Science Modified with Digital Arts and a minor in Human-Centered Design. I’m originally from Heredia, Costa Rica but I’m currently working from Boston as a Software Engineer II at Iterative Health. In my free time, I continue to practice my animation, design, and art skills. I am always looking for new ways to challenge myself to be more creative and put more effort into my projects.

    Outside the world of tech, you can find me skiing every week during the winter, going to camps with CISV around the world, volunteering at Coder Dojo, or baking chocolate chip cookies.
